The Loud House
Synopsis: Eleven-year-old Lincoln Loud resides with ten sisters. Lincoln, who has Clyde as his right-hand man, often comes up with inventive ways to get by in such a huge household. In the fictional Michigan community of Royal Woods, there are eleven children in Lincoln Loud's family, the only male and middle child.
Characters
Licoln Loud
Clyde Mcbride
The Loud House's primary protagonist is Lincoln Loud. He is also the middle child
On The Loud House, Clyde McBride is Lincoln's closest companion. He is an 11-year-old African-American boy, the adopted only child of Howard and Harold McBride, a same-gender couple. He is nerdy and smart.
